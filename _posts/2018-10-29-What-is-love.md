What is love exactly?

**For yourself**, love is:

1. Health
2. Freedom
3. Virtues of truth, kindness and beauty

**For parents**, love is:

1. Accompany
2. Listening
3. Financial support

**For wife**, love is:

1. Honesty
2. Devotion
3. Communication
4. Sex

**For kinds**, love is:

1. Education
2. Trust
3. Support

**For friends**, love is:

1. caring
2. selfless
3. trust

**For nation**, love is:

1. Loyalty
2. Gratefulness
3. Participation

**For human being**, love is:

1. Science and technology
2. Concern
3. Atition
